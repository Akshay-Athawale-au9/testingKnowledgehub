The Basics:

In this section we will discuss a brief walkthrough of the Product


  1. After successful onboarding, You will receive a mail as shown below, click on "Login Here" Button
  2. You shall get navigated to AECInspire login screen, where you can fill in Email & Password with emailed credentials
  3. Once your credentials gets validated, you'll be navigated to Dashboard
  • Your Projects: Represents all project counts you have access to.
  • Add New Project: Button for adding a new project.
  • Search: Helps search in your project list.
  • Sort: Sort projects by Newest, Oldest, Last Updated, etc.
  • Dashboard: Lists all accessible projects.
  • Projects: Manage project operations (Create, Edit, etc.).
  • Users: Manage user operations (Create, Edit, etc.).
  • Q&A & Knowledge Hub: Find answers to all your queries.
  • Notifications: Manage all notifications.
  • Profile: Edit profile information.
  • Project Profile: Edit or delete projects from the dashboard.
  • Go to Project Button: Navigate inside projects.
