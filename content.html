The Basics:

In this section we will discuss a brief walkthrough of the Product


  1. After successful onboarding, You will receive a mail as shown below, click on "Login Here" Button
  2. You shall get navigated to AECInspire login screen, where you can fill in Email & Password with emailed credentials
  3. Once your credentials gets validated, you'll be navigated to Dashboard
  4. Your Projects: Represents all project counts you have access to.
  5. Add New Project: Button for adding a new project.
  6. Search: Helps search in your project list.
  7. Sort: Sort projects by Newest, Oldest, Last Updated, etc.
  8. Dashboard: Lists all accessible projects.
  9. Projects: Manage project operations (Create, Edit, etc.).
  10. Users: Manage user operations (Create, Edit, etc.).
  11. Q&A & Knowledge Hub: Find answers to all your queries.
  12. Notifications: Manage all notifications.
  13. Profile: Edit profile information.
  14. Project Profile: Edit or delete projects from the dashboard.
  15. Go to Project Button: Navigate inside projects.
